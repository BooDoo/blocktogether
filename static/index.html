Block Together

An API app intended to help cope with harassers and abusers on Twitter. Users of Block Together can share their list of blocked users with friends. Those friends can choose to block all the same people, both now and on an ongoing basis.

This app will:
  • receive a list of the people you block
  • (optional) block additional people for you
  • (optional) share your block list with others
It will not tweet on your behalf.
